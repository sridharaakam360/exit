Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a subscription valid?

Subscriptions are valid for the duration specified in the plan, typically 1-12 months from the date of purchase.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. However, we do not provide refunds for unused subscription periods.

How can I change my subscription plan?

You can change your subscription plan at any time by selecting a new plan. Your previous plan will be replaced by the new plan, and the new billing cycle will begin immediately.

How do I get access to more exams?

Each subscription plan offers access to different exams. To access more exams, you might need to upgrade to a higher-tier plan with more comprehensive coverage.
